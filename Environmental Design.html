Environmental Design

Blender & Adobe Photoshop

While I consider myself proficient with Adobe Illustrator, I recognize room for improvement in creating logos that are both sleek and contemporary. Moving forward, I aim to draw inspiration from modern design trends to enhance my future projects. Environmental design has been a passion of mine, particularly highlighted during my participation in the Next Bright Idea at Enderun Colleges. My project centered on integrating traditional Filipino farming techniques with modern technology. It was a personal favorite, marking my first experience of receiving constructive criticism during the presentation, which helped me identify areas for improvement and growth.
